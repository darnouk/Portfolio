Wyoming Tourist Attractions Map

Below is one of the first maps I made in my academic career in the Cartography & GIS program at UW-Madison.



This neat map showcases the basic geographic features of the state of Wyoming including counties, major cities, lakes, rivers, and popular tourist attractions, natural and historic. It consists of a central map highlighting Wyoming and a locator map showing the state relative to the contiguous United States. As an avid lover of the outdoors and hiking, maps like this are what sparked my passion for cartography.

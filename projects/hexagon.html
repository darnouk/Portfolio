Healthcare Expenditures per Capita by State

Below is a hexagonal cartogram map depicting healthcare expenditures per capita by state.



In my naive opinion as an amateur cartographer, I have really come to love the hexagonal cartogram design. Oh, how often have states like Rhode Island and Vermont gone underrepresented in maps due to their size! Often described as "nature's perfect shape", the hexagon is my personal favorite shape if I had to pick one. While this kind of map only rolls around once every election cycle, I truly believe that it is one of the clearest ways to depict geographic data in which size is not a factor.

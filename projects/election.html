Historical U.S. Presidential Election Results

Explore U.S. presidential election results from 1976 to 2020.



This interactive web map visualizes nearly five decades of U.S. presidential election data, allowing users to explore voting patterns and results across all 50 states.
The map provides an engaging way to examine historical electoral trends and state-by-state voting behavior over time.

How to use the map:
• Select a year from the dropdown menu to view results for that presidential election
• Hover over any state to see detailed voting data, including candidate names and vote counts
• States are color-coded by winning party (red for Republican, blue for Democratic)
• Click on states to view additional information including vote percentages displayed in pie charts
